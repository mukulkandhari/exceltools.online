ℹ️ About Excel Columns

  • Base-26 System: Excel uses a modified base-26 numbering system
  • Letter Range: A-Z (26 letters) for single columns
  • Double Letters: AA-ZZ for columns 27-702
  • Triple Letters: AAA-XFD for columns 703-16,384

🔧 Excel Limits

  • Excel 2003: 256 columns (A to IV)
  • Excel 2007+: 16,384 columns (A to XFD)
  • Rows: 1,048,576 rows in modern Excel
  • Total Cells: Over 17 billion cells per worksheet
